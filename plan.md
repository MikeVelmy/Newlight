# Velmure Tech — Agency Website Blueprint (plan.md)

## 1. Project Overview & Objective
Build a premium, high-performance, responsive portfolio website for **Velmure Tech**, a modern web design and interface development agency. The design must look exceptionally elite, technical, and trustworthy, using an editorial minimalist style that balances technical precision with premium aesthetics.

## 2. Branding & Visual Identity
- **Agency Name:** Velmure Tech
- **Primary Palette:** Deep Slate Blue (Base backgrounds: `bg-slate-950`, containers: `bg-slate-900/50`)
- **Accent Palette:** Vibrant Cyan (`text-cyan-400`, `border-cyan-500/30`, `focus:ring-cyan-500`)
- **Typography:** Sleek sans-serif pairing (Geist or Inter for body, sharp geometric sans-serif for headers)
- **Imagery Strategy:** Premium, real-world dark minimalist tech photography and structural abstract images pulled directly via secure Unsplash source URLs. No fake metrics, no artificial stock people.

### Services Grid (`<section id="services">`)
A high-contrast grid featuring three core pillars, utilizing subtle gradients over deep slate panels:
1. **Interface Design (UI/UX):** Deep user-journey wireframes, high-fidelity responsive prototypes, and interactive user flows.
2. **Premium Engineering:** Fast Next.js/React development, clean TypeScript safety, and ultra-flat structural DOM compilation.
3. **Search Engine Architecture:** Comprehensive on-page semantic structures, technical Core Web Vitals optimization, and fully integrated JSON-LD data.

### Industry Specializations (`<section id="sectors">`)
An asymmetric grid showcasing Velmure Tech's dedicated architecture systems for high-impact commercial industries:
1. **Corporate & Enterprise Firms:** Focus on institutional credibility, secure data handling, and premium multi-page brand positioning.
2. **Real Estate & Property Development:** Focus on blazing-fast media/image rendering, interactive layouts, and high-conversion lead capture modules designed to book property viewings.
3. **Automotive Dealerships & Showrooms:** Focus on high-visibility inventory displays and direct local search optimization to increase physical showroom foot traffic.
4. **Travel & Destination Brands:** Focus on seamless booking pipelines, rich experiential UI elements, and deep local search visibility to capture active tourists.

### Our Growth Packages (`<section id="packages">`)
A high-end, three-column package grid that showcases the deliverables from our core service blueprint, completely omitting pricing to drive direct consultations. 
1. **Starter Tier:** Essential digital foundation. Includes 3-Page high-performance site architecture, fully mobile-responsive design, native on-page SEO integration, and custom secure contact forms.
2. **Growth Tier (Most Popular):** Local visibility & lead acceleration. Includes 5-Page expanded architecture, everything in Starter, full Google Business Profile optimization, zero-friction WhatsApp booking integration, and custom domain connection.
3. **Premium Tier:** Complete market domination & rapid deployment. Includes full-scale multi-page architecture, everything in Growth, priority 5-day accelerated delivery, 1 month of dedicated post-launch support, and initial review acquisition setup.
